2-Year Limited Warranty

Applies to Magicarpet aftermarket Shock Absorber products. Covers manufacturing defects under normal use.

Back to Products

What’s covered

  • Oil leakage caused by manufacturing defect
  • Loss of damping due to internal defect
  • Structural failure under normal driving conditions

What’s not covered

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Noise or vibration caused by mounts / bushings / other chassis parts
  • Damage from incorrect installation, impact, or accident
  • Lifted / modified vehicles or non-standard fitment
  • Off-road, racing, or commercial abuse beyond designed duty

How to make a claim

  • Email photos/videos + order info to info@isussys.com
  • Include vehicle model/year and mileage, installation date, and a short symptom description
  • We’ll review within 2–3 business days and confirm next steps (replacement / return / inspection)

Note: Keep your invoice/order confirmation and installation record for faster processing.
