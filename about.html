About iSUS

iSUS Technology is a technology-driven suspension company specializing in electronically controlled damping and intelligent suspension electronics.

We operate with a dual-brand strategy:

- iSUS Technology focuses on the OE and OEM market, providing engineering-grade suspension systems, e-dampers, and electronic control solutions that meet vehicle-level development and validation requirements.

- Magicarpet is dedicated to the aftermarket and off-road segment, delivering performance-oriented and user-focused suspension upgrade solutions for enthusiasts and professional users.

This clear brand positioning allows us to serve both original equipment manufacturers and aftermarket customers with tailored products, processes, and support models.
